NES Controller USB Hub

4-port USB 2.0 hub inside of an original NES controller. Buttons on the controller are still pressable, but they serve no function.

GameBoy Breakout Board

Simple breakout board for performing audio mods on Nindendo Gameboys, Gameboy Colors, and Gameboy Pockets. Makes wiring multiple line-outs much easier.
