Active Fantasy Sports

Active fantasy sports are like regular season long fantasy sports, only now an additional element of the game involves your ability to be physically active. In Active Fantasy Sports leagues, you and your friends (or enemies) get assigned physical activity goals each week. You can all get the same goal or you can customize / handicap individual goals. When team owners meet weekly physical activity goals, they earn privileges in their league (e.g., better waiver wire priority). When team owners fail to meet weekly physical activity goals, they lose privileges in their league (e.g., a player may be dropped from their roster). As in standard fantasy sports, League Commissioners can customize various aspects of the game.
